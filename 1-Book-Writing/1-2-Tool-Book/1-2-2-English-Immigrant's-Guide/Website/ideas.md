### Idea A — "Warm Notebook" (Scrapbook Stationery)
- **Design Movement**: Cottagecore meets modern editorial — warm, handcrafted stationery aesthetic
- **Core Principles**: Warmth, approachability, visual delight, bilingual clarity
- **Color Philosophy**: Warm cream (#FFF8F0) background, terracotta/coral accents (#E8735A), sage green highlights (#7AAB8A), soft charcoal text. Emotionally: safe, cozy, encouraging.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric card-grid with slight rotations on decorative elements; sidebar navigation with sticky chapter list; content cards with subtle paper texture
- **Signature Elements**: Dashed underlines for vocabulary terms; small leaf/sprout icons as section markers; warm drop shadows on cards
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Hover lifts cards gently; language toggle slides smoothly; dark mode shifts to warm dark brown (not cold black)
- **Animation**: Gentle fade-up on scroll; card hover scale 1.02; language switch cross-fade
- **Typography System**: Playfair Display (headings) + Nunito (body) — elegant yet friendly; Chinese: Noto Serif SC

- **Interaction Philosophy**: Crisp, no-nonsense transitions; language toggle as a physical switch; dark mode inverts to deep navy with yellow accents
- **Animation**: Slide-in from left on navigation; instant color transitions; no decorative motion
- **Typography System**: Space Grotesk (headings) + Source Sans 3 (body); Chinese: Noto Sans SC

## Selected Approach: **Idea A — "Warm Notebook"**

Chosen because it best balances the "cute but professional" requirement — warm enough for nervous newcomers, structured enough for graduate students. The scrapbook aesthetic is internationally legible and emotionally reassuring without being childish.
